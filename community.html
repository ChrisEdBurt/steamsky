Steam Sky

Roguelike in a sky with steampunk theme

Here are few ways to join Steam Sky community:

Chat

Steam Sky use one global chat room to communicate. This room can be accessed in few ways. Try to imagine normal room with many doors. Select one of the "doors" from list below.

Matrix room

Matrix is a communicator similar to Discord or Slack. Main the game chat room is Steam Sky at Matrix.org You may connect to it directly from your browser here:

Join Steam Sky chat room

IRC room

If you prefer to use IRC, you can connect to chat room from IRC too. Just point your favourite IRC client to

Steam Sky at Freenode.net

Gitter room

Gitter is communicator similar to IRC or Matrix, sometimes called IRC for developers. It is probably best place to follow game development from code perspective. You can enter Gitter room directly from your browser here:

Join Steam Sky Gitter room

Discord channel

And Discord text channel is available too. Just accept this invitation:

Join Discord Steam Sky channel

Chat archives

Full chat archives are available to everyone here

Forum

If you prefer forum, one is available here:

Join Steam Sky forum
